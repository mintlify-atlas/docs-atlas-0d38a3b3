---
title: "Notification troubleshooting"
description: "Fixes for common notification issues in Valstore, including background process restrictions on Xiaomi, OPPO, and Samsung devices."
---

<Warning>
  Some manufacturers (Xiaomi, OPPO, Samsung) aggressively kill background processes to save battery. If you are on one of these devices, follow the manufacturer-specific steps below in addition to the general fixes.
</Warning>

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Not receiving any notifications">
    Valstore requires notification permission to display alerts. If you have never granted this permission, or if it was revoked, notifications will not appear.

    <Steps>
      <Step title="Open Android Settings">
        Go to **Settings** > **Apps** > **Valstore**.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Open notification settings">
        Tap **Notifications**.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Enable notifications">
        Make sure **Show notifications** is turned on. You can also verify that individual notification channels are not blocked.
      </Step>
    </Steps>
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Notifications stop after some time">
    Android battery optimization can terminate Valstore's background WorkManager tasks, preventing the app from checking your store and sending notifications.

    <Steps>
      <Step title="Open battery settings for Valstore">
        Go to **Settings** > **Apps** > **Valstore** > **Battery**.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Disable battery optimization">
        Select **Unrestricted** or turn off **Battery optimization** for Valstore.
      </Step>
    </Steps>

    **Xiaomi-specific steps:**

    <Steps>
      <Step title="Open app info">
        Hold the Valstore app icon, then tap **App-Info**.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Change battery saver setting">
        Tap **Battery saver** and select **No restrictions**.
      </Step>
    </Steps>

    <Tip>
      Keep Valstore in your recent apps or add it to your device's battery whitelist for the most reliable background behavior.
    </Tip>
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="No notifications after phone restart">
    On some devices, background tasks are not rescheduled after a reboot unless the app has Autostart permission.

    <Steps>
      <Step title="Find Autostart settings">
        Go to **Settings** > **Apps** > **Valstore**. Look for **Autostart**, **Auto-launch**, or a similar option. The exact label varies by manufacturer.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Enable Autostart">
        Toggle Autostart on for Valstore. This allows WorkManager to reschedule background tasks after a restart.
      </Step>
    </Steps>

    <Note>
      On Xiaomi devices, Autostart is found under **Settings** > **Apps** > **Manage apps** > **Valstore** > **Autostart**.
    </Note>
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Notifications are delayed">
    Valstore's background tasks are scheduled by Android's WorkManager, which batches and defers work based on battery state, network conditions, and Android version. Valstore cannot control the exact time a task runs.

    To improve timeliness:

    - Disable battery optimization for Valstore (see above).
    - Keep the device connected to power or on a strong network when you expect your shop to refresh.
    - On Android 12+, check that no app standby bucket restrictions apply to Valstore.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Wishlist notification not firing">
    The wishlist check runs when the `ValStoreStoreRenewal` task executes — once per daily shop cycle. If the skin was added to your wishlist after the shop already refreshed, the notification will not fire until the next renewal.

    **Checklist:**

    - Confirm the skin is saved to your wishlist in the app before your shop resets.
    - Make sure your Firestore wishlist is synced (you should see the skin listed under your wishlist in the app).
    - Verify that background tasks are not being killed (see "Notifications stop after some time" above).
    - Re-authenticate once manually by opening the app, to confirm your session cookies are still valid.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

<Tip>
  Adding Valstore to your battery whitelist is the single most effective step for reliable notifications on restricted Android devices.
</Tip>
