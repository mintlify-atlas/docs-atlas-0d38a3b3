The Night Market is a limited-time Valorant event that gives you 6 personal skin offers at reduced VP prices. Your Night Market is unique to your account — the skins and discounts differ from other players.

<Warning>
  The Night Market is not always available. The tab only appears during active events. Outside of an event, the Night Market tab is hidden.
</Warning>

## How it appears

Valstore checks the `bonusStore` field in your storefront API response. When `bonusStore` is non-null, the Night Market tab appears in the shop. When the event ends, the field returns null and the tab is removed.

## What you see

Each Night Market offer card shows:

- **Skin name** and weapon type
- **Original VP cost** (full price)
- **Discounted VP cost** (event price)
- **Percentage reduced** — the `discountPercent` field from the API response

static Future<NightMarket?> getNightMarket() async {
  // Returns null if Night Market is not active

}

## Countdown

The Night Market offer includes a `durationRemain` value (in seconds) that Valstore displays as a live countdown so you know how long your discounts last.

## Background notifications

The **`NightMarketRenewal`** WorkManager task runs periodically to check whether the Night Market has become active. When the event starts, you receive a local push notification so you don't miss your discounts.

<Tip>
  Make sure Valstore has notification permissions and battery optimization disabled so the background task can reach you promptly when Night Market opens.
</Tip>
