Valstore is a portable Valorant shop viewer built with Flutter for Android. It authenticates with your Riot Games account via SSO and fetches your personalized shop data directly from Riot's APIs — no third-party servers involved.

## Features

- **Daily shop** — View all four skins in your rotating daily offer
- **Night market** — Check your discounted night market skins when the event is active
- **Bundles** — Browse skins that are part of the current featured bundle
- **Gallery** — Explore all Valorant weapon skins with pricing
- **Wishlist** — Save skins you want and get notified when they appear in your shop
- **Push notifications** — Background refresh via WorkManager alerts you when a wishlisted skin is available
- **Inventory** — View the skins you already own

## Project status

<Note>
  Valstore was previously available on the Google Play Store but has since been removed and the repository is archived. The app is fully functional and can still be built from source or sideloaded. It is released under the MIT license.
</Note>
